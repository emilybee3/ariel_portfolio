An Experimental Film by Ariel Sinelnikoff. 2014

Upon attempting to escape her puritanical father’s cabin, Mary begins to experience reoccurring paranormal activity that haunts her at night. What she unveils throughout her journey, however, is far more terrifying than the supernatural encounters she believes she is experiencing.

A 2014 San Francisco State University thesis film by Ariel Sinelnikoff.

A woman is unsuspectingly taken advantage of by her doctor in this 16 mm horror film.
